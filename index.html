About

I’m a chef with 10 years experience cooking in my client’s homes. My journey started years earlier when I began cooking elaborate family meals on the holidays, turning into a true passion that I live and breathe.

For me, cooking is a transformational experience, combining the scents, sounds and tastes of complex flavors of different regions and cultures. Allow me to share with you my broad knowledge of some of these unique and special cooking techniques that I have learned under world-renowned chefs.

Process

I bring my process into your home, infusing it with a warm and genuine feeling. I play music music, use fascinating cooking methods, and share my knowledge of food with you so you can see exactly how and why each food is cooked and styled the way it is, all in the comfort of your own home.

The Menu

I am a cook second and a nutritionist first. I am vegetable forward, utilizing seasonal ingredients, and modern cooking techniques. I will cook for you healthy meals that are bursting with flavor and richness for a truly memorable dining experience.


My specialty is whatever your palate can dream. I’ve cooked with chefs from across the world, from Mexico to the Mediterranean, including Asian and Indian cuisines.

Availability

I am available Sunday - Friday, from 4:30 PM to 10:30 PM. Check my calendar for available dates. Holiday availability will vary.


Cancellations must be made more than 7 days in advance of your reservation. Reservations cancelled less than 7 days in advance, will be charged the full tasting menu price

Provisions

I will provide all food and drink, and come prepared with my own pots, pans, and utensils. I can also supply dishware if requested.

Allergy

I will cook around any allergy, be it shellfish, egg, soy, legume, or nut allergies etc. Please provide me advanced notice in your booking.

Dietary

I can accommodate requests for vegetarian and gluten-free meals. I cannot, however, prepare vegan or kosher meals.
